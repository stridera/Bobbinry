# Notes & Research

Rich research tool with folders, tagging, entity linking, and a pinboard.
Designed for the messier side of worldbuilding — research clippings,
character sketches, half-formed scene ideas.

- Two panels: `shell.leftPanel` for the notes navigation tree and
  `shell.rightPanel` for chapter-scoped notes that follow the editor.
- Notes can link to other entities across bobbins (characters, locations, events).
