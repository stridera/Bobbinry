# Code of Conduct

We follow the spirit of the Contributor Covenant (v2.1). Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.
- Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.
- Assume good intent; prefer guidance over gatekeeping.

**Enforcement:** Report issues to conduct@bobbinry.com. Maintainers may warn, moderate, or remove participation to keep the community healthy.

For the full text of the Contributor Covenant, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
